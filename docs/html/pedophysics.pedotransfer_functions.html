pedophysics.pedotransfer_functions.particle_density module¶

pedophysics.pedotransfer_functions.particle_density.Schjonnen(clay, org, densorg=1.4, denspart=2.65, densclay=2.86)¶

Calculate the soil particle density using the Schjonnen model.

This function determines the particle density of soil based on the content of clay, organic matter, and specific densities of organic matter, soil particles, and clay. It implements the Schjonnen model (referenced year or name of the paper).

clayarray-like

Soil clay content

orgarray-like

Soil volumetric organic matter

densorgfloat, optional

Density of organic matter [g/cm^3]. Default is 1.4 g/cm^3.

denspartfloat, optional

Density of soil particles [g/cm^3]. Default is 2.65 g/cm^3.

densclayfloat, optional

Density of clay particles [g/cm^3]. Default is 2.86 g/cm^3.

pdarray-like

Soil particle density [g/cm^3].

P. Schjønning, R.A. McBride, T. Keller, P.B. Obour, Predicting soil particle density from clay and soil organic matter contents, Geoderma, Volume 286, 2017, Pages 83-87, ISSN 0016-7061, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.geoderma.2016.10.020.

>>> Schjonnen(25, 5)
2.606
